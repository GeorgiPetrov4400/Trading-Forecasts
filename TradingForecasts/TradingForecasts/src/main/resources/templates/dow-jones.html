Dow Jones Historical data

The Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA), Dow Jones or the Dow for short tracks the performance of 30 of the biggest companies in the US including Boeing, Intel, and Dow. It’s often used as a barometer for the overall performance of the country’s equity markets. Historically it’s one of oldest and most followed indices. The chart is a useful measure of US economic health. When open "long" position you buy US30 index and when you open "short" position you sell US30 index.
