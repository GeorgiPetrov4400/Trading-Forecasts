    EUR/USD Historical Data

    EUR/USD is the forex ticker that tells traders how many US Dollars are needed to buy a Euro. The Euro-Dollar pair is popular with traders because its constituents represent the two largest and most influential economies in the world. When open "long" position you buy euro (respectively sell dollar) and when you open "short" position you sell euro (respectively buy dollar).
